How to Support

Join us in empowering children with special needs through your generosity.

Why Your Support Matters

At FULL CIRCLE, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive, regardless of their abilities or circumstances. Our mission is to provide quality education, therapy, and vocational training to children with special needs, enabling them to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

However, we cannot do this alone. Many of our students come from families facing financial hardships, and their access to essential learning resources depends on the generosity of donors like you.

Your Zakat & Sadaqah Can Make a Difference

  • Provide scholarships for underprivileged special children
  • Fund specialized therapy sessions
  • Equip classrooms with learning aids and assistive technology
  • Support vocational training programs for self-sufficiency
