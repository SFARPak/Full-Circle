About our Foundetion

We Are On A Mission To Assist the Under-privileged

FULL CIRCLE is the culmination of an idea that took form in 2007 under the umbrella of an institute for Special Children. Initially termed “Rahein”,the program envisaged the provision of quality education and therapy for deserving under-privileged children with a particular emphasis on re- habilitating the female child. Due to financial constraints, the Rahein program was dis-continued in 2021 leaving 30 deserving, non-affording families with no support and guidance for their special children. With meagre resources, Full Circle came into existence in October 2021.

Since then, we have expanded our outreach program and now have 20 children who belong to the under-privileged class and for whom education & therapy have been “out-of-reach”. At Full Circle, these children are provided with education, cognitive, speech and physical therapy. Weekly outings to a park are organized for the children to experience the great outdoors and to engage in healthy sporting activities.
